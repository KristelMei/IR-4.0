	The scope of the topic on exploring the development of learning in Industrial Revolution 4.0 is comprehensive and wide-ranging. It focuses on key technologies such as cloud computing, the Internet of Things, augmented reality, and more in the field of education. The study aims to understand how these technologies are changing traditional learning models and educational systems. It discusses the advantages and
disadvantages of Industrial Revolution 4.0 and also looks at the effects of these advancements on teachers, students, and educational institutions, including the necessity of digital literacy, the function of instructors in a technologically advanced environment, and the possibility of ensuring that all people have access to high-quality education.
