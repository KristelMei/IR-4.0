II. Objectives of the topic
• To discuss the historical context of the Industrial Revolution.
• To give insight about the changing conditions created by the industrial revolution in Europe, United States, and Asia specifically in the Philippines.
• To identify the key technologies of Industrial Revolution 4.0.
• To assess the advantages and disadvantages of Industrial Revolution 4.0.
• To understand the relationship of human-computer interaction in Industrial Revolution 4.0.
• To develop general knowledge about emerging technologies.
